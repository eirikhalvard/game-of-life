  • infoAbout

    Game of Life is a cellular automata created by John Conway. The "game" consists of a 2D grid of cells, where each cell is eather alive or dead. To determine if a cell lives or dies the next generation, we have to look at it's eight neighbours. If the cell is dead, and it has three alive neighbours, it will be born next generation. If a alive cell has anything else than two or three neighbours, it will die next generation. The 2D grid is also infinite, meaning if you reach the end of the board, you will be "teleported" to the other side of the board, like pacman. This fact makes it natural to fold the 2D canvas into a Torus(donut-shape).
